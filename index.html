Hey, I'm Saalik Khan, founder of Khan Tutoring and a 2023 WACE graduate. I'm currently studying a Bachelor of Biomedicine at UWA.

What sets my approach apart is recency: I'm not teaching from years-old memory of the syllabus. I sat these exact exams recently, under the current system, and know firsthand what the content demands, where students typically lose marks, and how to structure answers that score well. Scoring high isn't about a lucky day — it's about understanding the content thoroughly, knowing your own pitfalls, and communicating ideas clearly. I bring that perspective directly into how I teach.

Over the past three years, I've tutored more than 100 students in both group and private settings, giving them the direction, motivation, and know-how to reach their goals. Having built that experience, I want to keep giving WA students the guidance they need to succeed and pursue whatever they're aiming for next.
